Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce

Instructions

  1. Boil water and cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, brown the ground beef.
  3. Add tomato sauce and simmer until cooked through.

    Classic Brownies

    Ingredients

    • Unsalted butter
    • Granulated sugar
    • Eggs
    • All-purpose flour
    • Cocoa powder

    Instructions

    1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a baking pan.
    2. Melt the butter and mix with sugar, eggs, flour, and cocoa powder.
    3. Pour the batter into the pan and bake for 25-30 minutes.

      Red Pasta

      Ingredients

      • Penne pasta
      • Olive oil
      • Garlic
      • Tomato sauce
      • Italian seasoning

      Instructions

      1. Cook penne pasta according to package instructions.
      2. In a pan, sauté minced garlic in olive oil.
      3. Add tomato sauce and Italian seasoning; simmer for 10 minutes.
      4. Toss cooked pasta in the sauce until well coated.
